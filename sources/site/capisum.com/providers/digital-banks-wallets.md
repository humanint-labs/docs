# Digital bank and wallet integrations

Route wallet top-ups, merchant payments, and digital banking payouts through one standardized API. Capisum adapters cover OPay, PalmPay, Kuda, Moniepoint, and other wallet and neobank rails in Nigeria.

Supported providers in this category include OPay, PalmPay, Kuda, Moniepoint, Paga, VFD Microfinance Bank, Providus Bank, and Wema Bank / ALAT. Capisum is an integration layer — it standardizes API requests and responses without replacing your provider accounts or holding funds.
